Projects – Our Recent Projects Around the World

Siraba School Partnership

LifeThought staff built a professional online presence for a new NGO in Bamako, Mali, including a LinkedIn profile and social media accounts (Facebook, Instagram) to raise awareness of the NGOs local projects and community impact. This will allow the Siraba School to showcase its community building project and continue their important work. Next steps: creating a service learning framework for the school!

#MakeADifference Campaign

In a world that emphasizes the importance of the individual and teaches young people to prioritize their own wishes over connection with others, the need to help young people understand their responsibility to be good citizens and work for positive change in their communities continues to grow. LifeThought International staff and volunteers created a series of learning activities to help young students (K-6) understand the importance of community belonging, showing kindness, expressing gratitude, and serving others. These activities also develop students’ English proficiency and interpersonal communication skills. The learning materials were piloted in #MakeADifference learning activities were piloted by English teachers in elementary schools in Chiriqui, Panama, counselors at a youth camp in Predeal, Romania, and English classes in Bamako, Mali. At the request of international teachers using the curriculum, LifeThought volunteers translated the materials into Spanish and French. Building Bridges

Following the successful launch of the #MakeADifference learning materials, LifeThought International staff are collaborating with The Peacemaker Project to create learning activities to help teens learn civil communication strategies, practice culturally sensitive dialogue, and engage in peacebuilding discussions. Watch for the launch of these new learning materials soon!

LifeThought International is dedicated to fostering global change by empowering people to manage their own grassroots social projects through targeted, skills-based mentorship.
